Projects for People

Rhiza Research is a small nonprofit that seeks out projects where we believe filling key technical capacity gaps can have a positive impact on the well-being of ecosystems and communities. Currently we have two ongoing projects:

The Subseasonal-to-Seasonal (S2S) forecasting project seeks to evaluate the efficacy of using subseasonal-to-seasonal forecasts for aiding in agricultural decision making.

The Power EQuality project aims to highlight the global disparity of access to high-quality electricity globally and propose solutions to improve power quality by coalescing a community around the issue, building open power-quality monitoring solutions, and working with specific utilities and policy-makers to improve power quality globally.
